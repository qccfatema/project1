the cox bazar


Cox's bazar Beach is located at Cox;s Bazar, Bangladesh, the longest natural sea beach in the world running 75 milesand it is the 5th longest beach. There are 3 main beaches in the cox's bazar town: Kolatoli, Sugondha and Laboni.
Kolatoli point is an important part of cox's bazar beach situated near Dolphine Moure of cox's bazar. There are many hotel and restuarent reside in that area many buses stop near this point. Sogondha point situated from the north of Kolatoli point. There is a burmese market that is famous in Cox's bazar. People of cox's bazar sell traditional saree and jewelary. They also sell various kinds of things made of jute. There are many sea food restuarents. Laboni point is another famous part of the beach. People mainly gather mostly here. This part is best for surf,jog,cycle and swim by the seasore.
