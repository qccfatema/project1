Saint Martin Island



Saint martin island is a amazing place in Cox's bazar to visit.Most people tend to visit the island in Fall, Winter and Spring. The journey is unique by itself.In winter, there are many bird are seen. They stay in the time of winter as the weather is not that cold in Saint martin. In saint martin , people can go scuba driving with experts to have an outstanding experience. Saint martin is also know for sea food. There are varieties of lobstars, squids, crabs, octopus, mussels and different types of fishes. In local restuarents, you can pick food by themself and they will cook it for you.
